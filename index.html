We are on a mission to build a simpler, smarter, collaborative world of licensing.

Through experiencing the challenges within the fragmented licensing and supply framework, while building a global manufacturing business, our founder set out to find a solution. He believed that in a fiercely competitive market, having transparency across - and therefore control of - the entire supply chain would result in an unbeatable advantage.

Fabacus was created to build and deliver that solution and revolutionise the entire industry.

Our team consists of experienced, visionary and executive leaders from across the retail and licensing arena, as well as highly successful professionals well-versed in the development of large-scale cloud-based systems. We have collaborated with industry leaders, from top tier licensors and agents, to global standards organisations like GS1, to worldwide logistics operators like DP World, in order to develop this proposition and create a transformational solution for all stakeholders.

Xelacore creates an industry-wide, universally trusted catalogue and data hub, securely connecting all stakeholders across the entire value chain. A modular, SAAS solution with an intuitive interface and proprietary data management engine that allows all stakeholders to operate on a single, unified collaborative platform.

Xelacore bridges the gaps within the current retail & licensing ecosystem by creating authenticated, enriched, universal product records that unlock opportunities, reduce risk and drive performance. Through our proprietary platform, Xelacore, we’re bringing a new era of agility, efficiency and opportunity to the licensing world.

The platform has been developed to redefine the way licensors, licensees, agents and retailers work together to better achieve their common goals. We set out to support the industry to help stakeholders develop and sell products that the consumer wants, which are accurately represented in whatever channel they exist, whilst ensuring the products are safe, genuine and manufactured in a socially-responsible way.
